Privacy and Security

Privacy Settings
Users have full control over their personal data. The platform allows users to access, edit, or delete their stored information at any time to ensure their privacy preferences are respected.
The platform maintains detailed activity logs to help users monitor their actions. These logs include account logins, bookings, and other interactions for transparency and security.
Security Settings
Users can update their account password at any time. The system enforces strong password requirements to protect accounts from unauthorized access.
Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security by requiring a verification code in addition to the password during login.
Additional Security Measures
All sensitive user data is encrypted during transmission and storage to prevent unauthorized access or breaches.
The application complies with local laws and regulations, including data privacy and environmental protection standards.
